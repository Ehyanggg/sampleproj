COSTA LEONA SERIES

Costa Leona Series is a series of stories created by Filipino Wattpad Author, Jonah Mae Pacala, known by her pen name, Jonaxx, in 2016 starting with the first series, Scorching Love. Costa Leona Series is composed of two generations, the 3rd Generation (which spans from series #1 to #8) and 2nd Generation (which spans from #9 to #14).

The series focuses on different stories revolving around the members of the Riego, Hidalgo and Mercadejas clan, all of whom resides in a fictional place called 'Costa Leona'. Each stories focuses on different problems but are related one way or another.

History of Jonaxx

Jonahmae Pacala, also known for her pseudonym, Jonaxx is currently the most followed in Wattpad with 5 million followers despite being inactive. She is also the first-ever filipino who was verified on Wattpad. Fans, known as JSLs address her as the 'Unbothered Queen Jonaxx'. Undeniably, Jonaxx is the most popular and bestselling Filipino romance novelist today. Last 2013, she won as Writer of the Year. On 2014, Jonaxx won Gawad Kasulatan and dubbed as the Summit Media Queen. She also bagged the Best Writer in the Watty's Best of the Best Awards 2017.
